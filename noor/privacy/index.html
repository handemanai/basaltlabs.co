Privacy Policy

Noor — Ramadan Companion

Effective February 26, 2026 · Basalt Labs LLC

Noor is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking. Your worship data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

Information Stored on Your Device

Noor stores the following data locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to Basalt Labs or any third party:

  • Fasting records — daily fasting status, suhoor and iftar times, notes
  • Prayer tracking — prayer completion status and timestamps
  • Quran progress — reading position, juz progress, verse bookmarks, and notes
  • App preferences — prayer calculation method, madhab, notification settings, Quran reciter preference, font size, and display options
  • Zakat calculation inputs — gold and silver prices entered by you for zakat estimation

You may delete all of this data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Location Data

Noor requests access to your device's location only while the app is in use to calculate accurate prayer times and qibla direction. Your location is:

  • Processed entirely on your device using the Adhan prayer time calculation library
  • Stored locally in your app preferences so prayer times can be displayed without repeated location requests
  • Never transmitted to Basalt Labs or any external server

You may deny location access at any time through your device's Settings. If denied, you can enter your location manually.

Audio Streaming

When you use the Quran audio recitation feature, Noor streams or downloads audio files from EveryAyah.com, a third-party Quran audio hosting service. When connecting to EveryAyah.com:

  • Your device's IP address is visible to their servers, as is standard with any internet connection
  • The surah and ayah numbers you are listening to are included in the request URL
  • No user identifier, account information, or personal data is sent
  • Downloaded audio files are cached locally on your device for offline playback

We do not control EveryAyah.com's server logs or data practices.

In-App Purchases

Noor offers optional premium features through Apple's App Store. All purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. Basalt Labs does not receive or store your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

Noor does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information (no account creation is required)
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprinting data
  • Usage analytics, session data, or behavioral tracking
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Health data (Noor does not use Apple HealthKit)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or calendar data
  • Push notification tokens (all notifications are scheduled locally on your device)

Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Exposed
EveryAyah.com Quran audio files IP address, requested surah/ayah
Apple App Store In-app purchases Managed entirely by Apple

Noor does not integrate any analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or social media SDKs.

Widgets and Live Activities

Noor provides Home Screen widgets and Lock Screen Live Activities displaying prayer times, fasting progress, and Ramadan information. This data is shared only between the Noor app and its own widget extensions on your device using Apple's App Group framework. It is not accessible to other apps or transmitted externally.

Siri and Shortcuts

Noor supports Siri Shortcuts for checking prayer times, fasting progress, and logging fasts. Data provided to Siri is processed by Apple in accordance with Apple's Siri Privacy Policy. Basalt Labs does not receive data from your Siri interactions.

Data Security

All data stored by Noor resides in your device's sandboxed app container, protected by iOS security measures including:

  • App sandbox isolation (other apps cannot access Noor's data)
  • Device encryption (when a passcode is enabled)
  • No cloud sync or remote backup of app data by Noor

Data Retention and Deletion

All Noor data is stored locally on your device. Basalt Labs does not retain any of your data on any server. To delete all data associated with Noor, simply delete the app from your device. All locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Noor does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since Noor does not collect personal information from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of Noor after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
